About Me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering at the University of Michigan (2022-present), where I work with Professor Rada Mihalcea in the Language and Information Technologies Group. Prior to that, I received my Master of Science in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University (2018-2021).

My current research interests include AI agents for user experience simulation, mental health AI systems, retrieval-augmented generation for healthcare applications, and large language model alignment. I'm particularly interested in developing AI systems that can provide emotional support, simulate realistic human behaviors, and assist in healthcare decision-making processes.
